What is LISA?

LISA is a trilogy of RPG Maker games made by Austin Jorgensen. The trilogy follows and explores the Armstrong family and their many many flaws.
The first game in the series, LISA: The First, follows Lisa Armstrong in a trippy nightmarish world. By the end of the game it becomes clear that Lisa was raped by her abusive father, Marty Armstrong. The first game is probably the least significant in the overall plot and it was the last game I played in the series.
The second and most popular game in the series, LISA: The Painful follows Lisa's brother Brad Armstrong.
