What is Khonjin House?


Khonjin House is a web series created by Khonjin. Khonjin House started in 2015 and ended in 2016, ended again in 2019, and finally ended in 2021 The series consists of short comedy videos that are seemingly unrelated. The Khonjin House universe is absolute nonsense and anything can happen.

My Experience


I think i discovered the series in 2016. The original Khonjin House movie was in my YouTube recommended and I decieded to watch it because of the thumbnail. Little did I know, I stumbled upon my favorite web series ever.

Major Characters

Khonjin


Khonjin is the main character of Khonjin House. He is involved in countless nonsensical antics throughout the series, however he is usually the cause of the antics.

Gino


Gino is the owner of Fratelli's Pizza, which is also called Gino's Pizza. Khonjin and Gino have a constant rivalry throughout the series.

Gay Spaghetti Chef


Gay Spaghetti Chef is the chef at Fratelli's Pizza. However he does not know how to make pizza, only spaghetti. Also he is gay! (duh)
